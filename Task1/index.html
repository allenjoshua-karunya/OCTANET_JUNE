About Us

Our journey began over two decades ago with a passion for classic cars and a commitment to preserving automotive history. Our Restored Vintage Cars has grown from a small garage to a renowned restoration workshop.

Our love for vintage automobiles, old engines and learning the intricacies of classic car mechanics served as the base for this venture.

At Retro Rides, we believe that every classic car has a story to tell. Our dedicated team of expert mechanics and craftsmen work tirelessly to bring these stories to life, restoring each vehicle to its original glory with meticulous attention to detail and authenticity. Whether it's a barn find or a family heirloom, we treat every project with the utmost care and respect.

Our mission is to keep the legacy of classic cars alive for future generations to enjoy. We invite you to join us on this journey, whether you're a seasoned collector or a first-time buyer, and experience the magic of vintage automobiles.
